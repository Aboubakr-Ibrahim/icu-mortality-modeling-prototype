The experiment writes:

- held_out_metrics.csv

- split_summary.csv
- mortality_models.mat when model saving is enabled

Before publishing any number, confirm:

1. Cohort authorization and provenance.
2. A fixed 24-hour prediction time.
3. No post-24-hour or post-outcome predictors.
4. Patient-level split independence.
5. Both outcome classes in every split.
6. Training-only imputation and scaling.
7. Validation-only threshold selection.
8. A locked held-out test evaluation.
9. Cohort size, prevalence, missingness, and exclusions.
10. Complete reporting of both baselines, calibration, limitations, and failures.

Results from the predecessor experiment must not be copied into this directory.
